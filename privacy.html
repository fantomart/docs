Privacy Policy for Whose Turn?

Last updated: 28 May 2026

Whose Turn? (the "App") is provided as a free utility. The App is designed to work entirely offline.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. It does not request access to your contacts, location, photos, microphone (audio is played only, not recorded), camera, or any other personal data.

Network Activity

The App does not make any network requests. It works without an internet connection.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. Since no personal data is collected, the App is fully compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.
